About Hasbulla
Hasbulla Magomedov was born and bred in Makhachkala, Dagestan. He took to social media during the height of the COVID-19 pandemic and began posting content to Instagram & TikTok where his notability slowly sprung. Early social media posts showed his hotheaded nature and the high jinks of his life. Later posts saw him sporting fighting gear, routinely boxing, striking & grounding opponents in the gym. Hasbulla is 19 years of age, although he has a babyface, childlike features and a high-pitched voice.
